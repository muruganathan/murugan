Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact you?
- For a quote use the form in the right side.
- Send email to sales@vegamsoft.com
What are the payment methods?
We can Accept payments through following methods. We will talk you through during our initial discussions.
Paypal, Wire transfer.
Online bank transfer(India, UK Only)
Demand Draft (India Only)
How will you deliver the website to me?
We will deliver the work based on your preference.
1. Host directly to your domain
2. Share it through dropbox / Google Drive
3. Send by email (if the size of the file is small enough)
Can I have the source code?
We do not restrict anything from our side. If proprietary codes / plugins etc. used then it has to be agreed with relevant supplier.
I’m a new client whom to talk to?
When you contact us we will assign you an account manager. He/she will be looking after all your project needs including delivery and support post delivery if regular support is agreed.
Can you give us a quote before starting the project?
Yes. We will give you free quote. No commitments at this stage. Note that final quote needs to be agreed post requirements / scope clarifications.
Do you have established software development lifecycle?
Yes. We use Waterfall Model. From delivery point of view it is Requirements > Design & Development > Testing > Go Live.
We have defined deliverables & templates to drive smooth software development cycle.
After completing the project, if I have any issues who should I contact?
You can contact your account manager. We will always be there for you.
Do you provide post project support?
Yes. This will be agreed separately.
